01. Who I Am

About Me

Hello! My name is Promila Kumari and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet (and in the classroom!). My interest in teaching started back in 2018 when I decided to try creating interactive learning experiences for students — turns out designing engaging lessons is pretty fun!

Fast-forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of working at an elementary school, a middle school, and a high school. My main focus these days is building accessible, inclusive learning experiences for students of all ages.

Here are a few technologies and tools I’ve been working with recently:

  • Smart Board Tech

  • Gamified Learning
